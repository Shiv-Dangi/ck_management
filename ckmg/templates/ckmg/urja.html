Mission of “URJA”

    The mission of Urja is to inspire, to uplift, to serve, to elevate, to educate and to encourage the morale, mindset and self confidence of students & professionals.

Necessity of “URJA”

    This concept of Urja is being developed from our other training and career counselling programmes. We have been in career counselling field since last five years and counselled around 4,000 students. We have observed that students have potentials, confidence, skills, talents and energy level to do any work but we found the problem in their attitude as well as found that they don’t have right guidance and directions for using these abilities. Due to this, our career counselling was not confined to career only. It was the combination of career counselling and mind programming.

    Hence, the primary purpose of making Urja is totally the welfare of students as well as guiding and inspiring them so that they can boost up themselves & to lead a successful and happy life.

    The Urja is for all students of schools and colleges as well as any human being. We will be spread Urja by our inspirational and theme based sessions, which will be addressed by experts of various fields. In addition to this we have FACEBOOK and WHATSAPP groups through which we spread Urja Daily through motivational messages.
