  • The Art of Mind Programming / Chetan Raikwar Success System
We are coming up with the new innovative concept of “Chetan Raikwar Success System” under the umbrella of CK Management Guidance.

Mission of Chetan Raikwar Success System

    The mission of Success Club is to inspire, to uplift, to serve, to elevate, to educate and to encourage the morale, mindset and self confidence of students & professionals.

Necessity of Chetan Raikwar Success System

    This concept of Success Club is being developed from our other training and career counselling programmes. We have been in career counselling field since last five years and counselled around 4,000 students. We have observed that students have potentials, confidence, skills, talents and energy level to do any work but we found the problem in their attitude as well as found that they don’t have right guidance and directions for using these abilities. Due to this, our career counselling was not confined to career only. It was the combination of career counselling and mind programming.

    Hence, the primary purpose of making this club is totally the welfare of students as well as guiding and inspiring them to lead a successful and happy life.

    The Success Club is mainly for students of schools and colleges. There we will be 3-4 inspirational and theme based sessions, which will be addressed by experts of various fields. Apart from it, there will be a library and books will be issued to students on fortnightly reissue basis. Every month magazines and current affairs booklet will be given to the students permanently.

Activities in Chetan Raikwar Success System

s. No. Activities Per Month
1 Inspirational Sessions 3-4

    Success mantras to achieve goals in life.
